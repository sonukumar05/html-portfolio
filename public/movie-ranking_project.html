The Best Movies According to Sonu

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception(2010)

Directed by Christopher Nolan, "Inception" is a mind-bending science fiction thriller. Dom Cobb, played by Leonardo DiCaprio, is a skilled thief who extracts valuable secrets from people's dreams. He is offered a chance to regain his old life by planting an idea into someone's subconscious, a process known as "inception." With a mesmerizing blend of stunning visuals and intricate storytelling, Cobb and his team dive into multiple dream layers, blurring the lines between reality and illusion. As the mission unfolds, they face unexpected challenges and thrilling twists that keep viewers on the edge of their seats.

The Conjuring(2013)

Directed by James Wan, this film serves as the foundation for the entire franchise. It follows paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren as they help a family terrorized by a dark presence in their farmhouse. The film is known for its atmospheric tension, effective scares, and compelling performances.

3 Idiots(2009)

A heartwarming comedy-drama, "3 Idiots" revolves around the friendship of three engineering students at an Indian college. The film tackles the pressure of academic expectations, highlights the importance of pursuing passion, and emphasizes the need for an alternative approach to education.
